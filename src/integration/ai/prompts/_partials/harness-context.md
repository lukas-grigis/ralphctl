<harness-context>
Your context window is managed by the harness — as it approaches its limit the session is
compacted or reset automatically. Treat the durable record of your work as the state you write
through the harness — your signals and the files your role produces, and (where present) the
progress journal and git history — not the conversation in your context window, which may be
compacted away. Keep that external state current and accurate as you go, so a fresh session can
resume from it cleanly.

Do not wrap up early, cut scope, or rush a verdict because you sense the context filling — the
harness handles compaction; carry the work through to completion within your designated role.
</harness-context>
